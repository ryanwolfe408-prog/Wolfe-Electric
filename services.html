Our Services

Residential and commercial electrical services for your home and business.

From panel upgrades and rewiring to EV chargers and lighting, we provide full-service residential and commercial electrical work. All work is performed by licensed professionals and backed by our commitment to quality.

  • Panel Upgrades

    Older homes often have panels that can't safely support today's power needs. We upgrade to modern, code-compliant panels so you can run appliances, HVAC, and electronics without worry.

  • Rewiring

    Full or partial rewiring for older homes with outdated or unsafe wiring. We minimize disruption and ensure everything is up to current codes.

  • EV Charger Installation

    Level 2 EV charger installation so you can charge your electric vehicle quickly and safely at home. We handle permits and electrical upgrades as needed.

  • Lighting

    Indoor and outdoor lighting design, installation, and repairs. Recessed lights, fixtures, dimmers, and landscape lighting.

  • Repairs & Troubleshooting

    When something's not working—outlets, switches, flickering lights, or tripping breakers—we diagnose and repair it quickly and correctly.

  • Safety Inspections

    Thorough electrical safety inspections for home buyers, sellers, or homeowners who want peace of mind. We identify hazards and recommend fixes.
